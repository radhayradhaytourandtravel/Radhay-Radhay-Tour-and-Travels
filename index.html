About Us

Welcome to RRT&T

Radhay Radhay Tours & Travels is your trusted travel partner for unforgettable journeys across Darjeeling, Sikkim, North Sikkim, Kalimpong, Dooars, and Siliguri. We specialize in personalized tours that showcase the natural beauty, culture, and charm of Northeast India. Whether you're seeking serene hill escapes or adventurous road trips, we've got you covered. Our well-maintained fleet includes Innova Crysta, Swift Dzire, Innova, Bolero, and Wagon R, ensuring comfort and safety throughout your travels. With a commitment to quality service and local expertise, Radha Rani Tours & Travels makes every journey smooth, memorable, and truly special. Let us drive your dream getaway!
